## Solving For Memswap

How to solve for MemSwap depends on the mode that the user or application chooses when submitting the order.

**Public Solving**

Public solving is discussed in detail in the Solver Integration . You can also checkout the [reference Reservoir solver](https://github.com/memswap-protocol/memswap/blob/main/src/solver/jobs/tx-solver-erc20.ts).

**Private Solving**

To become a private solver for the Reservoir Matchmaker, please reach out to the team.
