## Discovering Intents

Intents are propogated through [intentful transactions](/intentful-transactions).

In order to discover intents, solvers must listen to the Mempool, then parse memswap orders out of the transactions calldata

1. Listening to the Mempool
   [tx-listener](https://github.com/memswap-protocol/memswap/blob/main/src/solver/jobs/tx-listener.ts#L157): responsible for discovering intents from mempool transactions.

2. Parsing Memswap orders
   [tx-order-parsing](https://github.com/memswap-protocol/memswap/blob/main/src/solver/jobs/tx-listener.ts#L157): Parse intents from calldata using the known order format.
